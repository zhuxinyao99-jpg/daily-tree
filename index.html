What makes it different

Time as rings

Each year is a tree. Each entry adds growth. The older the tree, the more you can see the shape of that time in your life.

365 moments

One entry per day maximum. It forces you to find the single most important thing worth remembering.

Never forget a day

Sleeping trees remind you which years you were present. Archived years hold their shape forever, even when dormant.

A forest is personal by nature

No social feed. No shares. Just you and your growing archive. Every tree tells the story of a year.
